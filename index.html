About

I’m a senior postdoc at the University of Kentucky (UKY) chasing quasar outflows and tinkering with the Cloudy photoionization code. I’m obsessed with everything space—from dusty tori to screaming ionized winds—and I like to keep it fun: turning spectra (JWST, DESI, etc.) into stories about multi-phase gas while leveling up Cloudy’s atomic databases.

Interests: AGN Outflows Cloudy JWST Atomic data

Research

Galactic‑scale absorption outflows in quasars

Measuring energetics (Ė, ṗ, Ṁ) and chemical abundances (N, S) in systems like 3C298 using UV/optical spectra and photoionization modeling.

Cloudy atomic database updates (C‑like sequence)

Integrating R‑matrix levels and collisions, cross‑checking NIST/Chianti; propagating theoretical energies to line identification.
